ReadME:
Page 1:
1. First you put down your name, where its asked
2. then you click “done”, and then it gives you a message
3. When the message has been read you click “next”
Page 2:
This page will greet the user by the given name.
1. You give two of your chosen numbers where it is asked
2. Then you can calculate the two numbers however you like – by subtracting, adding,
multiplying, or dividing.
3. The answer will be shown red if its negative and if its green its positive
4. If you want to go back and give a new name you can click the “back” button in the top
left
5. when done calculating you can see the history by clicking the “history” button in the top
right
Page 3:
Here you can see the history of the user's calculations.
1. You can go back calculating by pressing the “back” button bottom left
2. If you are done you can press the “finished” button on the bottom right.
Page 4:
The last page will thank you for using the application.
1. You can go back by pressing the “back” button
2. Or you can go back to the beginning, by pressing “back to start” button
